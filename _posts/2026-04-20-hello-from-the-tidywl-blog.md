This is the TidyWL blog. Hello.

## Why a blog

TidyWL started as a one-evening hack because YouTube's Watch Later playlist had quietly eaten years of my attention. Building the extension involved reverse-engineering the internal InnerTube API, working around the 5,000-video cap, and discovering that "deleted" videos on YouTube mean a lot of different things under the hood.

Most of that context doesn't fit on the landing page — it belongs here.

## What to expect

- **Product updates** — new features, breaking changes, release notes.
- **Technical notes** — how TidyWL works, what I've learned about YouTube's internals, and things that are non-obvious but worth documenting.
- **The occasional opinion** — on playlist UX, privacy, or why YouTube hasn't fixed any of this in ten years.

No schedule. No newsletter funnel. Just writing when there's something to say.
